Our Programs

Comprehensive Islamic education pathways for every stage of learning — from foundational Quran recitation to advanced Islamic jurisprudence.

Full-Time Programs

Nazira — Quran Recitation

Foundation program in the correct reading of the Holy Quran

Boys Girls Full-Time

The Nazira program is the foundation of all Islamic learning. Students develop the ability to read the Holy Quran fluently and accurately, applying the rules of Tajweed (proper articulation and recitation). This essential program is a prerequisite for advancement into the Hifz or Alim programs.

Students are taught in small, focused groups by qualified teachers who ensure each student receives personalized attention. The program instills a deep love for the Quran and establishes the correct habits of recitation from the very beginning.

Rules of Tajweed (Makharij al-Huruf)
Complete Quran recitation from Alif to Ya
Memorization of short Surahs and essential Duas
Small class sizes for individualized attention
Separate classes for boys and girls
Foundation for all further Islamic study
⭐

Hifz — Quran Memorization

Complete memorization of the 30 Juz of the Holy Quran

Boys Girls Full-Time

The Hifz program is among the most honored journeys a Muslim can undertake — the complete memorization of the Holy Quran. At Darul Uloom New York, our Hifz students are enrolled in a structured, full-time program under the supervision of experienced Huffaz who have themselves memorized the Quran with Ijazah (certified authorization).

We provide a spiritually charged and disciplined environment where students can focus entirely on this sacred endeavor. Upon completion, graduates earn the title of Hafiz/Hafiza and join a distinguished lineage of Quran guardians.

Complete memorization of all 30 Juz
Daily revision and Sabaq (lesson) system
Qualified Huffaz teachers with Ijazah
Regular Dhor (revision) sessions
Separate boys and girls departments
Spiritual mentorship and character development
📚

Alim Course — Islamic Scholarship

Comprehensive multi-year program in Islamic sciences

Boys Girls Full-Time

The Alim Course is a comprehensive, multi-year program that covers the full spectrum of traditional Islamic sciences. Following the time-honored Dars-e-Nizami curriculum, students graduate as qualified Islamic scholars capable of serving their communities as Imams, teachers, and community leaders.

The curriculum is taught by highly qualified faculty trained at prestigious institutions around the world. Both boys and girls are offered this program with dedicated departments, faculty, and facilities.

Tafsir (Quranic exegesis)
Hadith sciences (Sihah Sittah)
Fiqh & Usul al-Fiqh (Islamic jurisprudence)
Arabic language, grammar & literature
Aqeedah (Islamic theology)
Seerah & Islamic history
⚖️

Iftaa Course — Islamic Jurisprudence

Advanced specialization in the science of issuing Fatawa

Boys Only Alim Graduates Only

The Iftaa Course is an advanced post-graduate program open exclusively to male graduates of the Alim program. It provides in-depth specialization in Islamic jurisprudence (Fiqh) and the science of issuing religious rulings (Fatawa).

Students train under experienced Muftis, engage with complex contemporary legal questions, and learn to apply the principles of all four Madhabs to modern issues. This is one of the most demanding and prestigious programs offered at the institute.

Advanced Fiqh from all four Madhabs
Training in writing and researching Fatawa
Contemporary Islamic finance & economics
Medical ethics in Islam
Direct mentorship by senior Muftis
Open only to Alim Course graduates
Part-Time Program

Maktab

After-School Islamic Education

The Maktab is our dedicated after-school program designed for children and youth who attend regular school during the day. Classes run in the afternoons and evenings, allowing students to receive quality Islamic education without sacrificing their general schooling.

Open to both boys and girls, the Maktab offers a warm and engaging environment where children learn to read the Quran, study the fundamentals of Islam, memorize essential Duas, and develop a strong Islamic identity and character.

Enroll a Child Ask a Question

Quran Reading (Nazira)

Learn to read the Quran with proper Tajweed in a step-by-step manner.

Islamic Studies

Fundamentals of Aqeedah, Fiqh, and the pillars of Islam and Iman.

Duas & Ahadith

Memorization of daily Duas, short Surahs, and selected Ahadith.

Basic Arabic

Introduction to the Arabic alphabet and foundational vocabulary.

Open to Boys & Girls

Separate classes for boys and girls in a safe, nurturing environment.

Flexible Schedule

Afternoon and evening sessions designed around school hours.
