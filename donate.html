Give for the Sake of Allah

Your sadaqah supports Islamic education, the Masjid, and students in need. Every contribution — large or small — makes a difference.

Why Your Donation Matters

Darul Uloom New York is a charitable institution that depends entirely on the generosity of the community to fulfill its mission. Every dollar donated goes directly toward educating the next generation of Muslim scholars.

Your Impact

$25/month Covers learning materials for a Maktab student
$100/month Sponsors a Hifz student for a month
$500 Supports an Alim student for a semester
$1,000+ Major donor — named scholarship opportunity
