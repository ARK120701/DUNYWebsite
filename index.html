Welcome to Darul Uloom New York

Darul Uloom New York is a distinguished institute of Islamic learning, committed to preserving and transmitting the sacred knowledge of the Quran and Sunnah to the next generation of Muslims in America.

We offer a comprehensive suite of full-time and part-time Islamic education programs for both boys and girls, taught by qualified scholars trained in the finest Islamic institutions worldwide.

Our Programs

From foundational Quran reading to advanced Islamic jurisprudence, we offer a complete path of Islamic scholarship.

Nazira

Foundation in proper Quran recitation with Tajweed rules. Students learn to read the Quran correctly and fluently with sound pronunciation.

Boys Girls
Learn More

Hifz

Complete memorization of the Holy Quran under the guidance of experienced Huffaz. A transformative spiritual and academic journey.

Boys Girls

Alim Course

A multi-year comprehensive Islamic scholarship program covering Hadith, Fiqh, Tafsir, Arabic language, and classical Islamic sciences.

Boys Girls

Iftaa Course

Advanced specialization in Islamic jurisprudence and the science of issuing Fatawa. For graduates of the Alim program seeking deeper mastery.
