About Us

Discover our story, our mission, and the values that have shaped Darul Uloom New York into a beacon of Islamic learning.

وَعَلَّمَكَ مَا لَمْ تَكُن تَعْلَمُ

Our Mission & Vision

Darul Uloom New York was founded on the firm belief that authentic Islamic knowledge is the cornerstone of a thriving Muslim community. Our mission is to provide the highest quality Islamic education that is simultaneously rooted in tradition and relevant to the modern world.

We are committed to cultivating scholars and community leaders who embody the Quran and Sunnah in their character, are equipped to serve their communities, and contribute positively to society at large.

View Our Programs

Our Mission

To provide authentic, high-quality Islamic education that nurtures students spiritually, morally, and intellectually — preparing them to serve their families, communities, and the Ummah.

Our Vision

To be the leading Islamic studies institution in New York, producing qualified scholars and informed Muslims who carry the torch of knowledge and exemplify the beauty of Islam.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to providing equal, high-quality Islamic education to boys and girls in a safe, nurturing, and spiritually enriching environment.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Darul Uloom New York.

Authentic Knowledge

We transmit knowledge in the traditional manner — teacher to student — through an unbroken chain reaching back to the Prophet ﷺ.

Community Service

We are committed to serving the broader Muslim community of New York and beyond, making Islamic education accessible to all.

Character Development

Beyond academics, we focus on developing the moral character, manners (Adab), and piety (Taqwa) of every student.

Inclusive Education

We believe both boys and girls deserve access to comprehensive Islamic education and provide dedicated programs for each.

Nurturing Environment

Our institute fosters a warm, supportive community where students feel safe, respected, and inspired to grow.

Relevance & Context

While honoring tradition, we equip our students to navigate the contemporary world with confidence in their Islamic identity.

Our Journey

A history of growth, dedication, and service to the Muslim community of New York.

Founded

Humble Beginnings

Darul Uloom New York was founded by a group of dedicated scholars and community members with a vision to bring authentic Islamic learning to the heart of New York.

Growth

Hifz Program Launched

The Hifz program was established, welcoming boys and girls to memorize the complete Quran under the care of dedicated and experienced Huffaz.

Expansion

Alim Course Introduced

The full Alim curriculum was introduced for both boys and girls, offering a comprehensive multi-year program in Islamic sciences.

Today

A Thriving Institution

Today, Darul Uloom New York serves hundreds of students across all programs, with a growing faculty of qualified scholars, and a vibrant community of learners.
