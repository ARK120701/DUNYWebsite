Our Faculty

Our distinguished scholars bring decades of experience and authentic knowledge from the world's leading Islamic institutions.

Institute Leadership

Guiding Darul Uloom New York with wisdom, scholarship, and dedication.

M

Mufti Muhammad Rashid

Muhtamim (Principal)
Institute Administration

Mufti Muhammad Rashid is a graduate of Darul Uloom Karachi and holds an Iftaa degree. With over 25 years of teaching experience, he leads Darul Uloom New York with a commitment to authentic scholarship and community service. He has authored several works in Islamic jurisprudence and is a respected voice in the New York Muslim community.

A

Sheikh Abdullah Rahman

Nazim al-Taleem (Academic Director)
Academic Affairs

Sheikh Abdullah Rahman graduated from Darul Uloom Deoband and pursued further studies in Hadith sciences in Madinah. He oversees the academic curriculum and faculty development at the institute, ensuring the highest standards of Islamic education for all students.

Boys Department Faculty
Y

Qari Yusuf Patel

Head of Hifz Department
Boys Hifz

Qari Yusuf completed his Hifz at age 12 and holds an Ijazah in the ten Qira'at. He has taught Hifz for over 15 years and has produced numerous Huffaz who serve as Imams across the country.

I

Maulana Ismail Siddiqui

Alim Course — Hadith & Tafsir
Boys Alim Course

Maulana Ismail is a graduate of Jamia Uloom Islamia, Allama Binnori Town, specializing in Hadith and Tafsir sciences. He teaches Bukhari Sharif and Tafsir Jalalayn at the senior level.

U

Mufti Umar Farooq

Iftaa Course — Islamic Jurisprudence
Iftaa Department

Mufti Umar completed his Iftaa training under senior scholars in Pakistan and the UK. He specializes in contemporary Fiqh issues including Islamic finance, bioethics, and family law.

H

Maulana Hasan Ali

Arabic Language & Grammar
Boys Alim Course

Maulana Hasan holds a degree in Arabic linguistics from Al-Azhar University, Cairo. He teaches Arabic grammar (Nahw & Sarf), rhetoric (Balaghah), and classical Arabic literature.

F

Maulana Farhan Qureshi

Nazira & Maktab Instructor
Boys Nazira & Maktab

Maulana Farhan is passionate about teaching beginners the beauty of Quran recitation. He has specialized training in Tajweed and brings energy and patience to his younger students.

Z

Maulana Zakariyya Hassan

Islamic History & Seerah
Boys Alim Course

Maulana Zakariyya brings Islamic history and the Seerah of the Prophet ﷺ to life in the classroom. He teaches with passion and aims to connect students deeply with their heritage.

Girls Department Faculty
K

Ustadha Khadijah Malik

Head of Girls Department
Girls Administration

Ustadha Khadijah is an Alima and Hafiza who completed her studies at a leading Darul Uloom in the UK. With over a decade of teaching experience, she leads the girls department with wisdom and compassion.

F

Hafiza Fatima Siddiqui

Head of Girls Hifz
Girls Hifz

Hafiza Fatima memorized the Quran at age 14 and holds an Ijazah. She has dedicated her career to helping girls complete their Hifz, combining firm discipline with genuine warmth and encouragement.

A

Ustadha Aisha Nawaz

Alim Course — Fiqh & Hadith
Girls Alim Course

Ustadha Aisha is a graduate of Darul Uloom Leicester and has pursued post-graduate studies in Hadith sciences. She teaches Fiqh, Hadith, and Islamic jurisprudence to the senior girls.

M

Ustadha Maryam Osman

Tafsir & Aqeedah
Girls Alim Course

Ustadha Maryam brings a deep love for the Quran to her teaching. She specializes in Tafsir and Islamic theology, helping students understand and internalize the meanings of the Quran in their lives.

R

Ustadha Ruqayyah Baig

Arabic Language
Girls Alim Course

Ustadha Ruqayyah studied Arabic language and literature in Jordan and brings a native-level fluency to her teaching. She focuses on making Arabic grammar accessible and engaging for students.

Z

Ustadha Zainab Rahman

Nazira & Maktab Instructor
Girls Nazira & Maktab

Ustadha Zainab is known for her patient and encouraging teaching style. She specializes in teaching Quran to beginners and younger students, instilling a love for the Quran from an early age.
